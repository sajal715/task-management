A Task Management System is a software tool designed to help individuals and teams organize, track, and complete tasks efficiently. It allows users to create, assign, prioritize, and monitor tasks in real time, ensuring better productivity and collaboration. By streamlining task handling, these systems improve efficiency, reduce missed deadlines, and enhance overall team coordination.
